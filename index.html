Improving the communication of construction details

Help us to improve how we communicate construction details

Thank you for taking the time to complete this BRANZ survey.

This brief questionnaire is intended to gain an understanding of preferences and requirements for the viewing of 3D and 4D construction details. We would be very grateful if were were to take a few minutes to review the different means of communicating the following sample details, and then answer the accompanying questions. Your feedback will inform how BRANZ communicates 3D and 4D illustrations to construction details in the future.

Your responses will remain confidential, and used solely for the purpose of this study.
